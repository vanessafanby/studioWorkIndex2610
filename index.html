Introduction Exercise

Hi, my name is Vanessa (she/her). I was born in China and moved to Melbourne when I was 4 years old. I choose Digital Media because I'm interested in filming and content creation. My favourite emoji is the classic smiley face, since it reminds me to always have a big smile on my face. The most comfortable software for me is Premiere Pro, since I used this the most for assignments, and to edit my own vlog videos. My previous coding experience is quite limited. I have completed only one semester of Interactive Media, where I learned the basic foundations of coding. I am excited to deepen my skills and further explore mediated expression in more advanced and creative ways.
